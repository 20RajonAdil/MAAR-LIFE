# MAAR LIFE

## Your Personal Life Operating System

MAAR LIFE is a premium personal life management platform designed to help individuals organize, track, and improve every major area of life from a single dashboard.

Unlike traditional productivity apps, MAAR LIFE combines health, discipline, finance, faith, study, goals, habits, and personal development into one unified system.

---

## Features

### Life Dashboard

* Comprehensive Life Score system
* Progress tracking across multiple life domains
* Daily action planning
* Personal growth analytics

### Goals & Planning

* Long-term goal management
* Daily action generation
* Progress tracking
* Milestone monitoring

### Habits & Discipline

* Habit tracking
* Streak system
* Daily consistency monitoring
* Performance insights

### Health & Wellness

* Health metrics tracking
* Exercise monitoring
* Nutrition management
* Personal wellbeing analytics

### Finance Center

* Income and expense tracking
* Financial planning
* Savings goals
* Personal budgeting

### Faith & Spiritual Growth

* Faith-based habit tracking
* Personal reflection tools
* Spiritual growth monitoring

### Learning & Development

* Study tracking
* Skill development planning
* Knowledge growth monitoring

### Timetable Management

* Custom timetable creation
* PDF timetable imports
* Calendar integration support
* Daily schedule organization

---

## Privacy First

MAAR LIFE is designed with privacy as a core principle.

* No accounts required
* No servers required
* No cloud storage required
* Data remains on the user's device
* Full local-first experience

---

## Technology Stack

* HTML5
* CSS3
* JavaScript (Vanilla JS)
* Chart.js

---

## Project Vision

MAAR LIFE aims to become a complete personal life operating system where users can manage every important aspect of life through a single elegant and unified platform.

The mission is simple:

> Small daily actions, consistently repeated, create extraordinary long-term results.
